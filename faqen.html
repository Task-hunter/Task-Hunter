Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you wanted to know about Task Hunter

1. What is Task Hunter?

It’s a digital concierge service – a personal online assistant that helps you with quick, repetitive daily tasks.

2. How do I submit a task?

You can use the form on the “Contact” page or send it directly via email or WhatsApp.

3. How long does it take to get a response?

Tasks are usually processed within a few hours. Tasks sent outside working hours are handled starting the next day.

4. What kind of tasks can I send?

Any digital task that can be done online: searches, bookings, emails, reminders, organization, etc.

5. Can I send a task on the weekend?

Yes, tasks can be sent anytime. However, resolution during the weekend may depend on the availability of external services.

6. What does “priority response” mean in the Premium plan?

Your tasks will be prioritized and you’ll get faster replies compared to standard plans.

7. How do I pay for a task?

Payments are made online, via Revolut, bank transfer or any other mutually agreed method.

8. What if I exceed 10 tasks per month?

You can upgrade to the Concierge Premium plan or request extra tasks for an additional fee.

9. Is my data safe?

Yes. Discretion and confidentiality are essential to this service. Nothing is shared with third parties.

10. Can I cancel a task after I’ve submitted it?

Yes, if the task hasn’t started yet. Just send a quick message with the note “Cancel”.
