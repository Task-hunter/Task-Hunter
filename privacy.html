Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how Task Hunter collects, uses, and protects your personal data when using our services or submitting tasks through this site.

1. What data we collect

We collect personal data you voluntarily provide when submitting a task: name, email, phone number, and task details. No sensitive data is required unless necessary for the task and with your prior consent.

2. Purpose of data collection

Your data is used exclusively to deliver requested services, communicate with you about tasks, send confirmations, and manage your requests efficiently.

3. Data security

We take appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. We never sell or share your data with third parties without your explicit consent, unless legally required.

4. Data storage duration

Your data is stored for as long as needed to provide the services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. User rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, send an email to taskhuter.digital@gmail.com.

6. Cookies

This website does not use cookies or tracking systems. If this changes, we will update this policy accordingly.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy. Continued use of the service after changes implies acceptance of the updated policy.
